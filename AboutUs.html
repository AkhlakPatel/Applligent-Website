About Us

your satisfaction is our commitment

Applligent Technologies is a trusted mobile app & web app development company in India. We are experts in creating mobile apps in iOS, Android, FLutter & Ionic Platform, & we are also experts in creating WebApps & Backend in MEAN/LAMP. We are the trusted development partners of many of our offshore clients from the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, France, Netherland, Ukraine, Nigeria, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Singapore & Australia. We are experts in delivering innovative & strategic mobility solutions for start-ups & enterprises to grow their business from scratch to the sky. Our skilled team consists of visionary leaders, critical thinking business analysts, creative designers, problem-solving developers & meticulous testers, who can help you build your vision into reality.Customer satisfaction is our utmost priority that’s why we are always on time & on budget.
